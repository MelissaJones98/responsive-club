About Us

Our founder, Melissa, grew up with a love of reading. When she was a young child, her mother would read to her from books with stunning illustrations, as well as short stories written by author Enid Blyton. Melissa adored hearing about woodland animals, fairies, elves and other magical creatures. Sometimes the worlds they lived in were mystical places far far away, other times the tales told of magic that is present in the real world but carefully concealed from sight. This fuelled young Melissa's imagination and ignited her passion for fantasy and reading, a passion which she carried into adulthood.

As she grew older, Melissa expanded her library with a vast range of books from lengthy novels to manga filled with gorgeous illustrations. She found new authors she enjoyed: Roald Dahl, J. K. Rowling, Stephen King, Andzrej Sapkowski, and many more... She explored various genres including horror, which she still loves, but nothing had the same magical affect as fantasy. Upon meeting other like minded people, Melissa decided to form a book club to create a community with whom she could share her passion. And thus The Witch's Book Nook was born!
